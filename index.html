About me:

I am a fifth-year PhD student in the ECE Department at Rutgers University under the supervision of Prof. Sheng Wei. My research interests include multimedia systems and artificial intelligence (AI), with a focus on improving the bandwidth efficiency of virtual reality (VR) streaming systems using deep learning and computer vision techniques. Also, I have conducted security related research to address various vulnerabilities in multimedia, AI, and hardware systems, as well as AI related research to support the next generation wireless communication network.
